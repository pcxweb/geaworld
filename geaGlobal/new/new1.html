GEA achieves highest possible rating for application and follow-up excellence.

We are delighted to share with you that GEA (Global Education Alliance) has become an officially authorized education agency of U.S. News Global Education - a premier higher education resource in the United States that assesses excellence in application and how well an agency ensures excellent outcomes of their students. The authorization has cemented GEA’s position as a top Education Agency.
The result puts GEA in a small group of Education Agencies which can claim to be genuinely elite – ranked highly for both application and follow-up excellence.
U.S. News Global Education is a premier higher education resource which aims to recognize, reward and improve excellent application and follow-up services offered by education agencies. It also aims to provide students with clear information to help them select the best-fit institution in the United States.
There are also outstanding achievements of our counselors.
With professionalism and a comprehensive knowledge of studying in the United States, all of GEA’s counselors in the U.S. Department have completed and passed the U.S. News Best Colleges Rankings training module. They have been awarded the Certificate as Trained Education Counselors.
